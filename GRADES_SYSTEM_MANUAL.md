# Grades System User Manual

## Overview

The Grades System provides a comprehensive platform for faculty to assign marks and for students to track their academic performance. Built around a structured Mid-exam system, it offers detailed grade management, performance analytics, and transparent academic tracking across different subjects and semesters.

## Grading Structure

### Mid Examination System
Each subject follows a **Mid-based evaluation** system:
- **Mid 1 Examination**: First mid-semester evaluation
- **Mid 2 Examination**: Second mid-semester evaluation

### Marks Distribution (Per Mid)
Each Mid exam consists of **40 marks total**:
- **Exam Component**: 30 marks (75% of total)
- **Assignment Component**: 5 marks (12.5% of total)
- **Other Activities**: 5 marks (12.5% of total)

### Grade Scale
The system uses a 10-point grading scale:
- **A+ (90-100%)**: 10 grade points - Outstanding
- **A (80-89%)**: 9 grade points - Excellent
- **B+ (70-79%)**: 8 grade points - Very Good
- **B (60-69%)**: 7 grade points - Good
- **C+ (50-59%)**: 6 grade points - Above Average
- **C (40-49%)**: 5 grade points - Average
- **D (35-39%)**: 4 grade points - Below Average
- **F (0-34%)**: 0 grade points - Fail

### Passing Criteria
- **Minimum Requirement**: Average of both Mids ≥ 15/40 (37.5%)
- **Good Performance**: 60% and above
- **Excellent Performance**: 80% and above

## For Faculty Members

### Getting Started

1. **Login** using your faculty credentials
2. Navigate to the **Faculty Dashboard**
3. Click on the **Grades** tab

### Prerequisites

Before assigning grades, ensure you have:
- Created classes in the **Timetable** section
- Students enrolled in your classes
- Selected the appropriate **Academic Period**

### Managing Grades

#### Step 1: Select Academic Period
- Choose the academic year and semester from the dropdown
- The system displays classes specific to the selected period

#### Step 2: Choose a Class
View your classes in an organized card layout showing:
- **Subject Information**: Code and name
- **Class Details**: Department, section, academic year, semester
- **Schedule**: Day of the week and period timing
- **Class Type**: Regular class or Lab designation

#### Step 3: Select Examination Type
Choose between:
- **Mid 1**: First mid-semester examination
- **Mid 2**: Second mid-semester examination

#### Step 4: Grade Management Interface

**Student Management Tools**
- **Search Functionality**: Find students instantly by name or roll number
- **Advanced Sorting Options**:
  - Sort by Student Name (A-Z, Z-A)
  - Sort by Roll Number (ascending/descending)
  - Sort by Total Marks (lowest to highest, highest to lowest)
  - Sort by Percentage (performance-based sorting)
- **Pagination Controls**: Navigate through large classes efficiently (10, 20, 50, or 100 students per page)

**Grade Entry Interface**
For each student, you can:
- **View Student Information**: Name and roll number
- **Enter Marks in Three Categories**:
  - **Exam Marks**: 0-30 (main examination component)
  - **Assignment Marks**: 0-5 (assignment/project component)
  - **Other Marks**: 0-5 (participation, quizzes, etc.)
- **Real-time Calculations**: Total marks, percentage, and grade automatically calculated
- **Individual Save/Cancel**: Save or cancel changes for each student independently

**Grade Display**
- **Current Grades**: View existing grades with breakdown
- **Grade Badges**: Visual grade representation (A+, A, B+, etc.)
- **Percentage Display**: Exact percentage with one decimal precision
- **Edit Mode**: Switch between view and edit modes seamlessly

#### Step 5: Save Grades
- **Individual Saving**: Save grades for each student separately
- **Validation**: System ensures marks are within valid ranges
- **Confirmation**: Receive feedback when grades are successfully saved

### Advanced Faculty Features

#### Large Class Management
- **Efficient Search**: Type student names or roll numbers for instant filtering
- **Performance Sorting**: Sort by marks or percentage to identify top/bottom performers
- **Batch Processing**: Handle hundreds of students with pagination
- **Quick Navigation**: Jump between pages and adjust view settings

#### Grade Analytics
- **Performance Overview**: See class performance at a glance
- **Grade Distribution**: Understand how students are performing
- **Comparison Tools**: Compare Mid 1 and Mid 2 performance
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor student improvement over time

## For Students

### Accessing Your Grades

1. **Login** using your roll number as both username and password
2. Navigate to the **Student Dashboard**
3. Click on the **Grades** tab

### Understanding Your Grades Dashboard

#### Overview Tab
**Academic Summary**
- **CGPA**: Cumulative Grade Point Average across all semesters
- **Total Credits**: Credits earned from completed courses
- **Current GPA**: Grade Point Average for the current semester
- **Semesters Completed**: Number of academic semesters

**Performance Visualizations**
- **GPA Trend Chart**: Line graph showing your academic progress over semesters
- **Grade Distribution**: Pie chart showing distribution of grades (A+, A, B+, etc.)
- **Semester Performance**: Summary cards for each completed semester

#### Semester View Tab
**Semester Selection**
- Choose from completed semesters using semester buttons
- View detailed performance for each academic period

**Subject Performance**
For each subject in the selected semester:
- **Subject Information**: Name, code, and credit hours
- **Grade Badge**: Visual grade representation with color coding
- **Percentage**: Exact performance percentage
- **Progress Bar**: Visual representation of performance level
- **Marks Breakdown**: Total obtained marks vs. total possible marks

#### Subject Details Tab
**Comprehensive Subject Analysis**
For each subject, view:
- **Overall Performance**: Grade, percentage, and grade points
- **Credit Information**: Subject credits and contribution to GPA
- **Mid Examination Breakdown**:
  - **Mid 1 Results**: Exam (30) + Assignment (5) + Other (5) = Total/40
  - **Mid 2 Results**: Detailed component-wise marks
  - **Individual Mid Performance**: Percentage and grade for each mid
  - **Component Analysis**: See performance in exams vs. assignments vs. other activities

**Grading Status Indicators**
- **Complete Grading**: Both mids graded and finalized
- **Partial Grading**: Only one mid completed or components pending
- **Pending Grading**: Awaiting faculty grade entry

#### Analytics Tab
**Performance Insights**

**Strengths Section (80%+ Performance)**
- Subjects where you excel
- Encouragement and recognition of good performance
- Suggestions for maintaining excellence

**Areas for Improvement**
- Subjects needing attention with specific recommendations
- **At-Risk Alerts**: Subjects below 37.5% (failing threshold)
- **Improvement Needed**: Subjects between 37.5-60%
- **Next Mid Requirements**: Calculations showing what's needed for passing/good grades

**Performance Analytics**
- **Subject Comparison Charts**: Bar graphs comparing performance across subjects
- **Grade Points Distribution**: Visual representation of grade points earned
- **Semester Status Overview**:
  - Passing subjects count
  - Subjects needing improvement
  - At-risk subjects requiring immediate attention

### Grade Calculation Understanding

#### Individual Subject Calculation
```
Subject Percentage = (Total Marks Obtained / Total Marks Possible) × 100
Total Marks = Mid 1 Marks + Mid 2 Marks (out of 80 total)
```

#### GPA Calculation
```
Subject Grade Points = Grade Points × Credit Hours
Semester GPA = Total Grade Points / Total Credit Hours
CGPA = Overall Grade Points / Overall Credit Hours
```

#### Passing Requirements
- **Pass**: Average of both Mids ≥ 15/40 (37.5%)
- **Good Standing**: 60% and above
- **Dean's List**: 80% and above

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Faculty Cannot Enter Grades**
- Verify correct academic period selection
- Ensure class exists in timetable system
- Check that students are enrolled in the class

**Students Not Seeing Grades**
- Grades appear only after faculty saves them
- Verify correct semester/academic period
- Check if you're enrolled in the subject

**Grade Calculations Seem Wrong**
- Verify all components are entered correctly
- Check that marks are within valid ranges (Exam: 0-30, Assignment: 0-5, Other: 0-5)
- Ensure both mids are considered for final calculation

**Search/Sort Not Working**
- Try partial names or roll numbers
- Check spelling and formatting
- Use different sort criteria
